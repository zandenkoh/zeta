What is a Persona?

A Persona is a custom AI profile with a unique name, description, and system prompt that defines its behavior. You can create personas to tailor Zeta’s responses for specific tasks, roles, or personalities.

How it works:

  • Name: Give your persona a unique name.
  • Description: Describe its purpose or personality.
  • Image: Add an image URL to visually represent your persona.
  • Section: Assign it to a category like Writing or Programming.
  • Context: Set a system prompt to guide the AI’s responses.
  • Public Option: Choose to share your persona with others.
